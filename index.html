About Rachel's Rise

Hi, I'm Rachel! My sourdough journey began as a passion project during the pandemic, and it has blossomed into a labor of love. Every loaf I create is a testament to patience, traditional techniques, and quality ingredients.

I use only organic flour, filtered water, and sea salt - letting time and natural fermentation work their magic. Each loaf undergoes a minimum 48-hour fermentation process, resulting in bread that's not only delicious but easier to digest and packed with nutrients.

Baking is my meditation, and sharing these loaves with you brings me immense joy. Thank you for supporting my small business and joining me on this delicious journey!
